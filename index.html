About

I am a research engineer at FAR.AI. My current career interests are in understanding and reducing future risks from AI's growing role in the world. Contact me at tom@tomhmtseng.com.

Most recently, I worked on finding adversarial attacks against superhuman Go AIs, demonstrating that superhuman capabilities in a domain do not automatically provide robustness.

Previously I was a software engineer at Gather and Cruise. As a student at MIT and Carnegie Mellon, I had the pleasure of working on parallel algorithms research with Julian Shun, Guy Blelloch, and Laxman Dhulipala.
